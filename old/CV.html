Sam Vaughan

Email: sam.vaughan@sydney.edu.au

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the University of Sydney, studying galaxy evolution and formation using a process called Integral Field Spectroscopy. I use large data sets and cutting-edge computing techniques to study the stars that galaxies are made of, how those stars are moving, and what this can tell us about the Universe we live in.
